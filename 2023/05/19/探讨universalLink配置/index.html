apple-app-site-association应该放在域名的根目录，举个例子，域名为：commonweb.bizgo.com，那么apple-app-site-association放置的地址为：

  • https://commonweb.bizgo.com/.well-known/apple-app-site-association
  • https://commonweb.bizgo.com/apple-app-site-association
    二者选其一，第一个地址优先，建议都配置 apple-app-site-association的内容$$
